Yoghurt Bowl with High-Protein Granola

Description

This yogurt bowl is a fresh and fruity delight, packed with protein to kickstart your day! Mix in our high-protein granola, toss in your favorite fruits, drizzle some nut butter, and boom—you’ve just made breakfast a total win!

Ingredients

Serves 1

  • Skyr Natural Yoghurt - 200g
  • High-Protein Granola - 30g
  • Strawberry - To serve
  • Blueberry - To serve
  • Kiwi - To serve
  • Peanut Butter - To serve

Steps

  1. Combine the yoghurt in a bowl alongside your homemade high-protein granola.
  2. Top with your choice of fresh fruit - we used kiwi, blueberries, and strawberries.
  3. Enjoy!
